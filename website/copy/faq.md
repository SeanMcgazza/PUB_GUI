# ChairTime FAQ

## Pricing & Commission

### Is ChairTime really free?
Yes. The free tier includes unlimited bookings, your own booking page, client database, and email reminders. No credit card required, no time limit.

We offer paid plans (starting at €12/month) for features like SMS reminders, team accounts, and advanced customisation. But solo stylists can run their entire business on free.

### Does ChairTime take commission on bookings?
Never. Zero percent. Not now, not when you get successful, not ever. This is a fundamental principle—we don't make money by taking yours.

Compare that to Fresha (they charge clients extra) or other platforms taking 20%+ of each booking. With ChairTime, a €50 appointment means €50 in your pocket.

### What do paid plans include?
- SMS appointment reminders (credits included)
- Team accounts for salons with multiple stylists
- Custom booking page colours and branding
- Priority support
- Advanced analytics

The free tier is genuinely useful—paid is for those who want extras.

## Setup & Getting Started

### How long does it take to set up?
10 minutes, maybe less. Add your services, set your hours, share your link. No training, no complicated onboarding. If you can post to Instagram, you can set up ChairTime.

### Do my clients need to download an app?
No. They just click your booking link and book through their browser. Works on any phone or computer. Nothing to install.

### Can I add appointments manually?
Yes. Walk-ins, phone bookings, or appointments made in person—add them to your calendar so online availability stays accurate.

## Data & Security

### Is my client data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted, stored on EU servers, and handled in line with GDPR. We never sell your data or your clients' data.

### Can I export my client list?
Absolutely. Export as CSV anytime. Your client relationships belong to you, not us. If you ever leave ChairTime, your data comes with you.

### Will you contact my clients?
Only for appointment-related messages (confirmations, reminders) that you've enabled. We never market to your clients or share their data.

## Features & Functionality

### Does ChairTime send reminders automatically?
Yes. Email reminders are automatic and free (default: 24 hours before). SMS reminders are available on paid plans—clients respond better to texts.

### Can I take deposits or payments through ChairTime?
Payment integration is on our roadmap. Currently, clients book and pay you directly however you normally handle payment (cash, card reader, bank transfer).

### Does ChairTime integrate with my calendar?
Yes. Sync with Google Calendar so appointments show alongside your personal events. iCal support for Apple Calendar users.

### Can I manage multiple locations?
Yes. Set different availability for different locations (e.g., salon Tuesday-Thursday, mobile Friday-Saturday). Clients see where you're available.

## Cancellation & Commitment

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no awkward phone calls. Cancel from your settings whenever you want.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?
You can export everything first. After cancellation, we retain data for 30 days (in case you change your mind), then delete it. Or request immediate deletion—it's your data.

## Who It's For

### Who is ChairTime designed for?
Independent hairdressers, small salons, barbers, beauty therapists, makeup artists—anyone who takes appointments for personal services and wants simple, commission-free booking.

### Who is ChairTime NOT for?
Large salon chains needing enterprise features, inventory management, or complex payroll integration. If you have 20+ staff across multiple locations, you probably need something like Phorest or Zenoti.
