# ChairTime How It Works

## Overview
Set up in 10 minutes. Share your link. Clients book themselves.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Profile
**What you do:** Add your name/salon name, upload a photo, write a short bio.

**Time:** 2-3 minutes

**Visual:** Profile card preview showing how clients will see you

**Why it matters:** Clients want to know who they're booking with. A friendly photo builds trust.

---

## Step 2: Add Your Services
**What you do:** List what you offer—cut, colour, styling, treatments. Add price and duration for each.

**Time:** 5 minutes

**Visual:** Service list builder with name, price, time fields

**Why it matters:** Clients see exactly what's available and how long to block in their day.

---

## Step 3: Set Your Availability
**What you do:** Mark your working hours, lunch breaks, days off. Block holidays or personal time.

**Time:** 2 minutes

**Visual:** Weekly calendar grid with toggles

**Why it matters:** Clients only see slots when you're genuinely available. No awkward declines.

---

## Step 4: Share Your Link
**What you do:** Copy your personal booking link. Add it to Instagram bio, WhatsApp status, business cards, wherever.

**Time:** 1 minute

**Visual:** Shareable link with copy button, social sharing icons

**Why it matters:** Every client who has the link can book. No app downloads required for them.

---

## Step 5: Clients Book Themselves
**What happens:** Client clicks link → sees your services and availability → picks a slot → confirms.

**Visual:** Booking flow from client's perspective (3 screens)

**Why it matters:** You don't do anything. The system handles it. You get a notification.

---

## The Flow (Visual Description)

```
[Profile] → [Services] → [Availability] → [Share Link] → [Clients Book] → [You Get Paid]
  2 min       5 min         2 min           1 min         (automatic)        💰
```

---

## What Happens After Booking

1. You get a notification (email/push)
2. Appointment appears in your ChairTime dashboard
3. Client gets confirmation email
4. 24 hours before: automatic reminder to client
5. Client shows up, you do great work, they pay you directly

---

## FAQ Tie-in
**"Do clients need to download an app?"**
No. They just click your link and book through their browser.

**"What if someone books a time I can't do?"**
They can't. Your availability settings prevent this.

**"Can I manually add appointments?"**
Yes. Walk-ins, phone bookings—add them yourself so your online availability stays accurate.
