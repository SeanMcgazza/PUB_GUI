# ChairTime Features → Benefits

## Feature 1: Zero Commission
**Feature:** No cut taken from any booking, ever

**Benefit:** A €50 cut and blow-dry stays €50 in your pocket. Over a year, that's thousands kept instead of given away.

**Proof point:** 0% commission vs 20%+ industry average

---

## Feature 2: Online Booking Page
**Feature:** Personal booking link clients can access 24/7

**Benefit:** Clients book at midnight, during their lunch break, whenever suits them. You wake up to a full diary without lifting a finger.

---

## Feature 3: Automatic Reminders
**Feature:** Email reminders sent before appointments (SMS optional)

**Benefit:** Fewer no-shows means more income and less frustration. Clients appreciate the reminder; you appreciate showing up to actual appointments.

---

## Feature 4: Service Menu Builder
**Feature:** Create your service list with prices and durations

**Benefit:** Clients see exactly what you offer and how long it takes. No confusion, no "how much for a balayage?" messages.

---

## Feature 5: Availability Control
**Feature:** Set working hours, breaks, days off, holidays

**Benefit:** Clients only see slots you're actually available. No more "Sorry, I'm off that day" messages.

---

## Feature 6: Client Database
**Feature:** All client details stored and searchable

**Benefit:** Remember preferences, see booking history, add notes. "The usual?" becomes possible.

---

## Feature 7: Google Calendar Sync
**Feature:** Appointments appear in your personal Google Calendar

**Benefit:** One calendar for everything. See work and life together without double-booking yourself.

---

## Feature 8: Data Export
**Feature:** Download client list and booking history as CSV

**Benefit:** Leave anytime with your client data intact. No platform lock-in.

---
