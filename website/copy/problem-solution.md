# ChairTime Problem → Solution

## The Problems

### 1. Booking platforms take a cut of your earnings
**Pain:** Fresha, Treatwell, and others charge commission on every booking. Work hard, build your client base, and watch 20%+ go to a platform that just... made a website.

**Solution:** ChairTime never takes commission. Not now, not ever. You keep 100% of what you charge. We make money from optional premium features, not from your sweat.

---

### 2. Clients book at bad times (or don't book at all)
**Pain:** Phone calls while you're mid-colour. Messages at midnight. "Can you fit me in?" when you're fully booked. Clients give up and go elsewhere.

**Solution:** ChairTime shows your real-time availability. Clients book themselves into open slots. No back-and-forth, no missed opportunities. Works while you sleep.

---

### 3. No-shows kill your income
**Pain:** Client doesn't turn up. That's an hour of income—gone. Calling to remind people feels awkward and takes time you don't have.

**Solution:** Automatic appointment reminders by email (SMS optional). Clients get nudged 24 hours before. Fewer no-shows, fuller books, less stress.

---

### 4. Your client list is trapped in someone else's system
**Pain:** Build your clientele on a platform, then realise you can't take those contacts with you if you leave. The platform owns your relationships.

**Solution:** ChairTime lets you export your client data anytime. CSV download, take it wherever. Your clients are *your* clients.

---
