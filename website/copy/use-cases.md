# ChairTime Use Cases

## Use Case 1: The Independent Stylist
**Who:** Emma left a salon to go freelance. Rents a chair three days a week and does mobile appointments the other days.

**Situation:** Building her own client base from scratch. Can't afford 20% commission to booking platforms when margins are tight.

**How ChairTime helps:**
- Free tier with unlimited bookings—no commission eating her profit
- Sets different availability for salon days vs mobile days
- Personal booking link on her Instagram bio drives new clients
- Automatic reminders reduce no-shows

**Result:** Keeps 100% of earnings. Books filled without paying for leads. Looks professional from day one.

---

## Use Case 2: The Small Salon
**Who:** Grace runs a 3-chair salon in Galway with two other stylists.

**Situation:** Using pen-and-paper booking. Phone rings constantly. Double-bookings happen. Staff availability is confusing to track.

**How ChairTime helps:**
- Each stylist has their own calendar within the salon account
- Clients pick their preferred stylist when booking
- Real-time availability prevents double-booking
- Quiet evenings become productive—clients book while salon is closed

**Result:** Fewer phone interruptions during appointments. Clear schedule for everyone. More bookings overall.

---

## Use Case 3: The Mobile Hairdresser
**Who:** Aoife does home visits—elderly clients, new mums, people who can't get to salons.

**Situation:** Coordinates appointments across different locations. Needs buffer time for travel. Hates spending evenings texting back and forth about availability.

**How ChairTime helps:**
- Sets buffer time between appointments for travel
- Blocks geographical areas on certain days for efficiency
- Booking link shared in local Facebook groups and WhatsApp
- Clients book themselves; Aoife just checks her schedule each morning

**Result:** More efficient routes. Less admin. Time back in the evening.

---

## Use Case 4: The Barber
**Who:** Damo runs a one-man barbershop in Dublin. Walk-ins are fine, but regulars want to book ahead.

**Situation:** Loses clients who can't get through on the phone. Walk-in busy times leave regulars waiting.

**How ChairTime helps:**
- Regulars book their weekly slot online
- Walk-ins still welcome—manually added to fill gaps
- "Booking ahead" becomes a perk for loyal customers
- Simple service menu: fade, cut, beard trim

**Result:** Regulars locked in. Walk-ins fill the rest. Phone stops ringing during cuts.
