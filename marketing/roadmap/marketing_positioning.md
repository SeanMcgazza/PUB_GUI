# ChairTime Marketing Positioning

## Core USPs

1. **Zero Commission** — Flat monthly fee, not percentage of bookings. Your revenue stays yours.
2. **No Contracts** — Cancel anytime. We earn your business monthly, not through lock-in.
3. **Beautiful Design** — Your booking page looks premium, matches the beauty industry aesthetic.
4. **Client Data Ownership** — Your clients, your data, exportable anytime.
5. **Built for Independents** — Designed for solo stylists and small salons, not enterprise chains.

---

## Target Audience Profile

### Primary: Independent Stylists & Small Salons
- **Business size:** Solo or 1-5 stylists
- **Location:** Ireland and UK initially
- **Age:** 22-45
- **Work setup:** Own salon, chair rental, or mobile
- **Current booking:** Instagram DMs, WhatsApp, paper diary, or Fresha/competitors
- **Tech comfort:** High (uses social media daily, comfortable with apps)

### Secondary: Growing Salons
- **Size:** 5-15 staff
- **Need:** Team scheduling, multiple service providers
- **Pain point:** Current system too basic or too expensive

### Psychographics
- Creative, values aesthetics
- Independent, entrepreneurial mindset
- Frustrated by platforms that take cuts
- Wants professional appearance without corporate feel
- Values relationships with clients
- Active on Instagram, visual-first

## Key Pain Points Addressed

| Pain Point | How ChairTime Solves It |
|------------|------------------------|
| Commission fees eating profits | Flat monthly pricing, zero commission |
| Booking via DMs is chaotic | Dedicated booking page, instant confirmations |
| No-shows cost money | Automated reminders, optional deposits |
| Generic booking pages | Beautiful, customisable design |
| Trapped by current platform | No contracts, data export |
| Admin eats into evenings | Automated booking, less back-and-forth |
| Clients can't see availability | Real-time calendar visibility |

## Competitor Comparison Points

### vs Fresha
- ChairTime: €29/month flat, zero commission
- Fresha: "Free" but 20% commission on new clients + payment processing fees
- ChairTime: Your client data, your relationship
- Fresha: Marketplace model, clients belong to platform

### vs Treatwell
- ChairTime: Direct booking, you control the relationship
- Treatwell: Marketplace takes commission, competes for your clients

### vs Timely/Vagaro
- ChairTime: Simpler, more affordable, no feature bloat
- Them: Complex, expensive tiers, built for bigger operations

### vs Paper Diary / DMs
- ChairTime: Professional, automated, always available
- Manual: Time-consuming, error-prone, unprofessional

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- **Warm:** Friendly, supportive, not corporate
- **Empowering:** You're in control of your business
- **Beautiful:** Aesthetics matter, we get it
- **Honest:** No hidden fees, no tricks

### Language
- "Stylist" and "salon" — industry-appropriate terms
- Speak to independence and ownership
- Reference real money (€50, not "significant savings")
- Acknowledge the hustle of running a beauty business

### Visual Identity
- Clean, modern, premium feel
- Soft colours that complement beauty/salon aesthetics
- Real photography over stock images when possible
- Mobile-first design showcases

### Don't
- Look corporate or enterprise
- Use aggressive sales tactics
- Ignore the creative/artistic nature of the industry
- Be generic — this isn't for everyone, it's for stylists

### Do
- Celebrate independence
- Show real numbers and savings
- Highlight beautiful design
- Speak like a peer, not a vendor
