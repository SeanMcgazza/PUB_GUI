## Conversion Tips

### Speed
- Target <3 second load time
- Compress all images
- Lazy load below-fold content

### Mobile Optimisation
- 60%+ of salon owners browse on mobile
- Large, tappable buttons
- Single column layout
- Test on actual phones

### Copy Guidelines
- Write for time-poor salon owners
- Short sentences, clear benefits
- Avoid jargon
- Use "you" and "your" often

### Trust Elements
- Real customer photos (not stock)
- Specific numbers ("60% fewer no-shows")
- Irish company badge
- Security/GDPR mentions

### Reduce Friction
- No credit card for trial
- Email-only signup option
- Social login (Google)
- Progress indicator in signup
