# ChairTime Launch Strategy

## Soft Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Stylist Friends (Weeks 1-2)
- Direct outreach to stylists in personal network
- Target: 5-10 active users
- Goal: Core booking flow validation
- Feedback: Video calls and direct messages

### Phase 2: Closed Beta (Weeks 3-6)
- Instagram outreach to independent stylists
- Target: 30 users
- Focus: Solo stylists frustrated with current solutions
- Goal: Refine onboarding, test edge cases, gather visual testimonials

### Phase 3: Open Beta (Weeks 7-12)
- Public launch with "early access" positioning
- Target: 100 users
- Enable referral program
- Goal: Prove retention, validate pricing

---

## Build-in-Public Tactics

### Weekly Rhythm
- **Monday:** Goals for the week
- **Friday:** What shipped + learnings
- Screenshots, progress clips, honest updates

### Content Hooks
- "Building the anti-Fresha" — position against commission model
- Behind-the-scenes of design decisions
- Feature polls — let potential users shape the product
- Share stylist feedback openly

### Engagement Strategy
- Follow and engage with stylists on Twitter/Instagram
- Respond to complaints about competitors
- Retweet/share stylist content
- Build relationships before selling

## First 100 Users Strategy

### Direct Outreach (Users 1-20)
- Personal network
- Instagram DMs to independent stylists
- Facebook groups for salon owners
- Local salons (in-person conversations)

### Social Proof Building (Users 20-50)
- Collect testimonials and screenshots
- Case study: "How [Stylist] saves €X/month"
- Before/after: DM chaos vs clean booking

### Content + SEO (Users 50-80)
- "Best Fresha alternatives" content
- "How to reduce no-shows" guides
- Instagram content showcasing booking pages

### Referral Program (Users 80-100)
- "Give 2 weeks free, get 2 weeks free"
- Easy sharing with pre-made messages
- Feature referrers on social

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Stylists don't trust new platform | Heavy social proof, transparent pricing |
| Low Instagram engagement | Collaborate with stylist accounts |
| Fresha loyalty | Focus on commission pain, show real savings |
| Feature requests overwhelming | Prioritise ruthlessly, stay simple |
