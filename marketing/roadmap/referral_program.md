# ChairTime Referral Program Design

## Program Overview

**Goal**: Leverage salon networks - salon owners know other salon owners.

**Program Name Options**:
- "ChairTime Friends"
- "Refer a Salon"
- "The ChairTime Circle"

**Tagline**: "Know a salon owner? Get them sorted. Get rewarded."

---

## Incentive Structure

### Recommended: Free Month Both Sides

| Party | Reward | When |
|-------|--------|------|
| Referrer | 1 free month | When referee pays first month |
| Referee | 1 free month | Applied to first month (trial extends) |

**Why this works**:
- High perceived value (€29-49)
- Simple to understand
- Aligned incentives
- Triggers on real conversion

### Alternative: Cash Credit

| Party | Reward |
|-------|--------|
| Referrer | €30 credit |
| Referee | €30 credit |

---

## Program Mechanics

### For Referrers
1. Log into ChairTime dashboard
2. Go to "Refer a Friend" section
3. Get unique link: `chairtime.ie/r/SALONNAME`
4. Share via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, in person
5. Track referrals in dashboard
6. Get free month when they convert

### For Referees
1. Click referral link
2. See: "[Salon Name] invited you to ChairTime"
3. Sign up for extended trial (14 days + 30 days free)
4. When they pay, referrer gets their free month

### Tracking
- Unique referral codes per salon
- 30-day attribution window
- Manual override for verbal referrals

## Program Rules

### Eligibility
- Referrer must be paying customer (not trial)
- Referee must be new to ChairTime
- Both must be real salons (fraud prevention)

### Reward Conditions
- Referee must complete trial AND pay first month
- Credits applied within 7 days
- Credits not cashable or transferable

### Limits
- No cap on referrals (encourage top performers)
- Annual review of program economics
- Right to modify/end program

### Fraud Prevention
- Same device/IP detection
- Manual review for high-volume referrers
- Real salon verification

## Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Week 1-2)
- Enable for top 50 most engaged salons
- Gather feedback

### Phase 2: Full Launch (Week 3)
