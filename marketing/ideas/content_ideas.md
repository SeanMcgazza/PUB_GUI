## Thread Ideas

### Thread 1: "The Hidden Cost of 'Free' Booking Platforms"
- They're not free, you pay in commission
- Calculate annual cost for average salon
- What happens to new client relationships
- The platform owns the customer, not you
- Alternative: pay flat fee, own everything
- CTA: There's another way

### Thread 2: "What I Learned Talking to 50 Stylists"
- Started building ChairTime, needed to understand
- Common frustrations across the board
- What existing tools get wrong
- What actually matters to stylists
- How it shaped our product

### Thread 3: "5 Signs Your Booking System is Costing You Clients"
- Booking takes more than 30 seconds
- Clients have to wait for confirmation
- No online booking at all
- Confusing service menu
- No reminders = no-shows
- How to fix each
