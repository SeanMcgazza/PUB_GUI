## Posting Guidelines

### Frequency
- **Twitter**: 1-2x daily
- **Instagram**: 1x daily (feed) + 3-5 stories
- **LinkedIn**: 2-3x weekly
- **Facebook**: 1x daily

### Best Times (UK/Ireland)
- **Instagram**: 9am, 12pm, 7pm
- **Twitter**: 8am, 12pm, 5pm
- **LinkedIn**: 8am, 12pm
- **Facebook**: 9am, 1pm, 7pm

### Engagement Rules
1. Reply to all comments within 4 hours
2. Like and comment on salon content
3. Share/repost customer content (with permission)
4. DM response within 24 hours
5. Monitor brand mentions daily

## Content Dos and Don'ts

### Do
- Use real salon photos when possible
- Share customer wins and testimonials
- Be helpful, not salesy
- Show personality
- Engage with beauty industry trends
- Use beauty industry language

### Don't
- Post only promotional content
- Ignore comments or DMs
- Use overly corporate language
- Post low-quality images
- Criticise competitors directly
- Share without visual content
