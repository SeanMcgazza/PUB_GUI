## Bidding & Targeting

### Target CPAs
- Brand terms: €3-5
- Salon software: €25-40
- Location-specific: €20-30
- Problem-aware: €30-45

### Budget Allocation
- Brand: 10%
- Salon software: 45%
- Location: 25%
- Problem-aware: 20%

### Geographic
- Primary: Ireland (all)
- Secondary: UK (exclude London initially)
- Bid adjustments: +15% Dublin, Cork, Galway

### Device
- Mobile: +10% (salon owners often search on phone)
- Desktop: baseline
- Tablet: baseline

### Time
- 7am-9pm: baseline
- 9pm-7am: -20%
- Weekends: baseline (salon owners research on weekends)
