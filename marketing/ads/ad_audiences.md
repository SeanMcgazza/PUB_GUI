# ChairTime Ad Audience Targeting Guide

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary Persona: "Solo Sarah"
- **Role**: Self-employed hair stylist / beautician / nail tech
- **Age**: 25-45
- **Location**: Ireland or UK (suburban/town, not central London)
- **Business**: Works alone, maybe chair rental
- **Tech comfort**: Uses Instagram daily, comfortable with apps
- **Pain points**: Hates phone interruptions, loses clients to no-shows
- **Current solution**: Paper diary, WhatsApp, phone calls

### Secondary Persona: "Growing Grace"
- **Role**: Salon owner with 2-5 staff
- **Age**: 30-50
- **Pain points**: Staff scheduling chaos, inconsistent bookings
- **Current solution**: Basic shared calendar, outdated software

## Audience Exclusions

### Always Exclude
- Current paying customers
- Employees
- People who searched "salon near me" (clients, not owners)
- Competitors (if identifiable)

### Consider Excluding
- Age under 21 (still in college)
- London (expensive, test separately)
- Interests in "getting hair done" (indicates client, not pro)

---

## Retargeting Strategy

### Funnel Stages

**Top (Awareness)**
- Audience: Blog readers, video viewers 25%+
- Ad type: Educational, brand intro
- Goal: Get to website

**Middle (Consideration)**
- Audience: Pricing visitors, feature page visitors
- Ad type: Social proof, feature highlights
- Goal: Start trial

**Bottom (Decision)**
- Audience: Trial page abandoners, demo viewers
- Ad type: Urgency, objection handling, offer
- Goal: Complete signup

### Retargeting Windows
| Window | Audience | Message |
|--------|----------|---------|
| 3 days | Hot leads | Strong CTA, "finish signup" |
| 7 days | Warm leads | Testimonial, benefits |
| 14 days | Cooling | Different angle, objection handling |
| 30 days | Re-engage | New feature, fresh offer |
| 60 days | Win-back | "We've improved", discount |

- Focus:
  - Northern England

1. Ireland (prove product-market fit)
2. Northern Ireland (cultural similarity)
3. UK regional cities
4. London (only with higher budget)

## Testing Framework

### Phase 1: Audience Discovery (Week 1-2)
- 4-5 different audiences
- Same creative
- €20-30/day each
- Goal: Find best performing audience

### Phase 2: Audience Refinement (Week 3-4)
- Layer targeting on winners
- Test exclusions
- Compare broad vs narrow

### Phase 3: Scale (Ongoing)
- Increase budget on winners
- Build lookalikes from converters
- Monitor for audience fatigue

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| CPC | <€1.50 |
| CTR | >1.5% |
| Landing page CVR | >5% |

## Platform-Specific Notes

### Instagram
- Strongest platform for beauty professionals
- Focus on visual content, Reels
- Story ads perform well
- Use beauty/salon hashtags in organic content

### Facebook
- Better for older demographic (35+)
- Groups (salon owner groups) for research
- Detailed targeting still works better here

### Google
- Capture high-intent searches
- Lower volume but higher intent
- Good for competitor terms

### TikTok (Future)
- Growing platform for beauty content
- Younger salon owners
- Test when ready to scale
