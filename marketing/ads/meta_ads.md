## Budget & Bidding

### Initial Budget
- €40-60/day across ad sets
- Minimum 3 days per ad before judging

### Bid Strategy
- Start: Lowest cost
- After learning: Cost cap at target CPA
- Target CPA: €25-35 for trial signup

### Placement
- Feed (Facebook + Instagram)
- Stories
- Reels
- Automatic initially, optimise after data

---

## Creative Testing Plan

### Week 1-2
- Test all 5 concepts
- Same audience
- Find winner by CTR + conversion

### Week 3-4
- Take winner
- Test variations (images, headlines, copy length)

### Ongoing
- Refresh every 4-6 weeks
- Seasonal angles (Christmas rush, summer weddings)
- UGC when available
