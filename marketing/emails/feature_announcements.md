## Announcement Best Practices

### Timing
- Major features: Tuesday or Wednesday, 9am GMT
- Minor updates: Any weekday
- Seasonal: 4-6 weeks before season

### Segmentation
- Power users: Early access, beta invites
- New users: Focus on core features
- Inactive: Use as re-engagement hook

### Follow-Up
- Day 3: "Have you tried [feature]?" to non-openers
- Day 7: Usage stats if applicable

### Subject Line Formulas
- 🆕 [Feature] is here - [Benefit]
- We built what you asked for
- [Feature]: Now live in ChairTime
- Your salon just got an upgrade
- [Emoji] Big news for your bookings
